Fluffy Banana Pancakes

‚Üê Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/3 milk

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mash a banana, then add the egg and mix it in.
  2. Add cinnamon, sea salt, flour, and baking powder.
  3. Add milk to thin the mixture
  4. Add mixture to a greased pan and cook as regular pancakes.
  5. Enjoy!
