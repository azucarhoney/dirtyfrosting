Our Story

Baking has always been more than just mixing flour, sugar, and butter—it’s a way to create warmth, joy, and connection.

My love for baking started in my high school cooking class.

This class inspired me to get in my family kitchen, where I spent countless hours perfecting recipes, experimenting with flavors, and sharing sweet treats with family and friends.

What began as a simple passion quickly turned into a dream: to one day open my own bakery, a place where every bite feels like home.

Dirty Frosting is my way of bringing that dream to life, one recipe at a time. This space is filled with my favorite homemade creations, from comforting classics to delightful new twists.

Every recipe is made with care, love, and the hope of inspiring others to find joy in baking.

One day, I hope to welcome you into my own bakery—a cozy spot filled with the aroma of freshly baked goods, a place where every cake, cookie, and pastry tells a story.

Until then, I invite you to explore my recipes, bake along with me, and share in this journey. After all, the sweetest dreams begin with a dirty kitchen and a little bit of frosting.
