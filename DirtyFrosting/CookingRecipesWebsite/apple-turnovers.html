Apple Turnovers

← Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 box of puff pastry
  • 2 apples + lemon juice
  • Cinnamon and nutmeg
  • 3/4 cup of water
  • 3 tablespons cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (185°C).
  2. On low heat pour water and cornstarch, and mix to thicken.
  3. Turn up to medium heat, pour in the sugars, and mix.
  4. Add in a dash of cinnamon and nutmeg.
  5. When caramelized, add in apples and leave on medium to low heat.
  6. Cut puff pastry into 12 squares and add filling to 6 squares.
  7. Close pastries, cut slits on top for ventilation.
  8. Brush tops with eggwash and bake for 20 minutes.
  9. Enjoy!
