Sweet Cornbread

← Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup of yellow cornmeal
  • 1 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 3 tablespoons heavy whipping cream
  • For honey butter:
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (185°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, cornmeal, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and sugars.
  3. In a seperate bowl, mix eggs, buttermilk, and heavy whipping cream.
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
  6. When done baking, brush the top with honey butter.
  7. Enjoy!
