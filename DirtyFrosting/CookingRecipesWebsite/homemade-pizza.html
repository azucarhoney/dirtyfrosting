Homemade Pizza Crust

← Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 500 g (3 3/4 cups + 2 tablespoons) all-purpose flour
  • 375 g (1 1/2 cups + 2 tablespoons) water
  • 1 g (1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 10 g (1 1/2 teaspoons) fine salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (185°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, water, yeast and salt.
  3. Knead dough in standmixer.
  4. Form shape of pizza and prebake.
  5. Add sauce, cheese and toppings.
  6. Bake.
  7. Enjoy!
