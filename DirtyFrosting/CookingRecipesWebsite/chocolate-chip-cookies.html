Soft Chocolate Chip Cookies

← Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons (1 stick) of melted unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 cup chcolate chips + and/or other add-ins

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (185°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat melted butter, sugar, and vanilla until creamy.
  4. Add in the egg and mix into wet mixture.
  5. Gradually add flour mixture into wet mixture and fold into the batter.
  6. Add chocolate chips and/or other add-ins.
  7. Roll dough into balls.
  8. Place on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper.
  9. Bake for 9-12 minutes.
  10. Enjoy!
