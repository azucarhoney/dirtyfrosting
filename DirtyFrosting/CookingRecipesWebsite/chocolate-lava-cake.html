Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 6 oz bittersweet chocolate (70% cocoa)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter and flour for greasing ramekins

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease two 6-ounce ramekins with butter and dust with flour.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter and chocolate together in 30-second intervals, stirring in between until smooth.
  3. Whisk in the powdered sugar until combined, then add the eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. Stir in the flour and a pinch of salt until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared ramekins, filling them about 3/4 full.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-14 minutes, or until the edges are firm, but the center is still soft. The cake should slightly pull away from the sides of the ramekin.
  7. Remove from the oven and let sit for 1-2 minutes before running a knife around the edges to loosen the cake.
  8. Carefully invert the ramekin onto a plate and serve immediately with vanilla ice cream or fresh berries.
