English Muffins

← Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon instant dry yeast
  • In a seperate bowl:
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Cornmeal for dusting

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350º (185º C).
  2. Bloom warm water, yeast, and sugar for 10 minutes.
  3. Pour all ingredients into a standmixer and knead for five minutes.
  4. Oil bowl and proof for one hour.
  5. On a floured surface, knead with hands for another five minutes, adding flour as needed.
  6. Spread out the dough into a square shape.
  7. Using a cup cut out 10-12 muffins.
  8. Sprinkle cornmeal on both sides of english muffin.
  9. Cook on a griddle until both sides are golden.
  10. Finish baking for 10-13 minutes.
  11. Enjoy!
